Salty Cedars Guest Suite & Ocean Adventures

Unforgettable Adventures located on the wild west coast of Vancouver Island

Our Guest Suite

Salty Cedar Guest Suite is centrally located in Ucluelet and is tucked away, nestled in a forest of ferns and cedar trees. It is a 2 minute walk from the most beautiful coastline trail, Black Rock Resort, Big Beach and the accompanying rugged beaches. We are close to town (5-7 minute walk) but with all the nature around us, it does not give that impression. Relax inside with fully heated floors, a large walk in shower, fast fibre wifi and high vaulted ceilings.

Ocean Adventures

At Salty Cedar Ocean Adventures, nestled on the stunning west coast of Vancouver Island, we invite you to embark on unforgettable ocean adventure. You'll be shuttled from our Guest Suite to our boat in the inner harbour of Ucluelet. From there you'll embark on a specialized ocean adventure to suit your needs. Our fishing charters are designed for both novice and seasoned anglers, offering you the chance to reel in species like salmon, halibut, and lingcod in some of the richest fishing grounds on the Pacific. Experience the thrill of the catch while surrounded by the majestic beauty of the Pacific Northwest. Beyond fishing, our ocean adventures include exhilarating whale watching tours where you can witness the awe-inspiring sight of humpback whales breaching, orcas swimming gracefully, and playful porpoises in their natural habitat. Each trip with us is not just an outing but a journey into the heart of nature, where the deep blue sea meets the rugged coastline, promising memories that will last a lifetime. Once back on land we will pack and prepare your catch so you can take it home with you.

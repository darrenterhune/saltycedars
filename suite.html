Our Guest Suite

Salty Cedar Guest Suite is centrally located in Ucluelet and is tucked away, nestled in a forest of ferns and cedar trees. It is a 2 minute walk from the most beautiful coastline trail, Black Rock Resort, Big Beach and the accompanying rugged beaches. We are close to town (5-7 minute walk) but with all the nature around us, it does not give that impression. Relax inside with fully heated floors, a large walk in shower, fast fibre wifi and high vaulted ceilings.

Situated amongst the salty cedar trees, our guest suite is modern, custom-built and family-friendly. It conveniently has its own entrance around the back, an outdoor space for lounging, and an area to rinse wetsuits-perfect for surfers and beach goers alike. Inside, enjoy the heated floors, king-sized bed, 48" T.V., high speed fibre wifi and west coast decor to keep you feeling cozy on those stormy nights. The kitchenette boasts a seamless-stainless steel counter top and sink, mini fridge, toaster, coffee maker, microwave and kettle. There is no stove/oven in our suite. There's a large walk in shower with a rainwater faucet and the essentials to keep you feeling fresh and clean. We are located close to town but you'd never know it thanks to our long driveway and beautiful surrounding rainforest.
